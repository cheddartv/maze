Personality

Personas are fictional characters, which you create based upon research in order to represent the different user types that might use our product in a similar way. Creating personas will help us understand our users‚Äô needs, experiences, behaviors and goals. Creating personas can help you step out of yourself. It can help you to recognise that different people have different needs and expectations, and it can also help to identify with the user we are designing for. Personas make the design task at hand less complex, they guide the ideation processes, and they can help to achieve the goal of creating a good user experience for our target user group.
